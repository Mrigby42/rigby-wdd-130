Hotsprings

One of the most unique things about this area is the plethera of hotsprings! This is something you won't find in Alaska thats for sure! There is an extensive list for sure, but heres a few to get you started.

  • Sunbeam hotsprings (right off the main road headed to Sunvally)
  • Mountain Village hotsprings (This is a natural hotsprigs inside a nice building )
  • Rocky Mountain hotsprings (This is for paying customers of Rocky Mountain ranch)
  • Boat Box (This is a unique hotsprings inside of an abandoned boat anchor, right off the river)
  • Iron Creek (This one is one only locals know about, and is quite hidden. If you are interested you can ask some locals to give you directions;)

Mountain Biking

If you are into mountain biking, thsi is the place to come! There are hundreds of trails all over the Sawtooths! The difficulty varies by trail, but there are easy green routes all the way up to blackdiamond difficult routes. A few that we recommend are:

  • Corral Creek (6.7 miles--Intermediate)
  • Greenhorn to Imperial loop (10.8 miles--Intermediate)
  • Fishhook trail (4 miles--Beginner)
  • The big smokey loop (57.3 miles--Difficult)
